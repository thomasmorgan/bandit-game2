Instructions

In this experiment, you have to look for treasure hidden in different countries. Within each country, the treasure will be hidden in a single location.

At the start of each trial you may be able to check some of the locations. For instance, you may be allowed to check five locations. You can check a location by clicking on it, if the treasure is there you will see a treasure chest, if it is not there you will see a red "X"
