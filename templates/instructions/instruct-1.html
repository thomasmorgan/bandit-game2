Instructions

In this experiment, you have to look for treasure hidden in different countries. Within each country, the treasure will be hidden in a single location.

On every trial you can check some of the locations and you can make your final decision at any point by clicking the "Ready to Decide" button.

You can check a location by clicking on it, if the treasure is there you will see a treasure chest, if it is not there you will see a red "X"
